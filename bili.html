Bilibili Monitor

This dashboard helps managers spot YouTube videos that have not been re-uploaded to Bilibili yet. For each managed creator we look at their five most recent YouTube videos and check whether each one also appears on their Bilibili channel. A green ✓ means the video is already on Bilibili; a red ✗ means it is still missing — a reminder to ask the creator to cross-post. Status refreshes once a day at 09:00 Beijing time.
